Frequently asked

Rivian Guides can typically add a referral code after ordering as long as your vehicle hasn't been delivered yet. Contact Rivian support through the app or website as early as possible and ask them to apply it before delivery. Once delivered, the code can no longer be added.

Points are generally credited within two weeks of your vehicle being delivered. You'll see them appear in your Rivian Rewards account inside the Rivian app. If they haven't appeared after a few weeks, contact Rivian support with your order details.

Yes. Points currently expire two years after being added to your account. Plan your redemptions before then — unused points are forfeited at expiration.

Complimentary charging is linked to your Rivian account after delivery — no separate activation required. Plug into any Rivian Adventure Network charger during the qualifying period and billing is handled automatically. The 3-month period starts from delivery, not from your first charge session.

The code applies to qualifying R1S, R1T, and R2 orders. Points vary by configuration — Tri and Quad Motor earn the most (500), Dual Max and Large earn 250, Dual Standard earns 100. The 3 months of free charging applies across qualifying configurations regardless of points tier.
