The R2 will likely be the smarter buy for most buyers. It delivers Rivian design, software, charging access, and off-road capability at a significantly lower price point.

The R1S earns its premium if you regularly use the third row, tow heavier loads, travel long distances, or fully utilize its off-road capability and air suspension system.

In real-world ownership, seating and cargo flexibility are usually more important than acceleration numbers.
