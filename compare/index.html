The R2 will likely be the smarter buy for most households. It delivers Rivian styling, software, and capability at a dramatically lower price point.

The R1S justifies its premium if you regularly use the third row, tow heavy trailers, travel long distances, or genuinely use the additional off-road capability.

The biggest deciding factor is usually seating and space — not acceleration.
