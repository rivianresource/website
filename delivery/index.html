Exterior
Full walk-around in good light
0/14
Panel gaps consistent all around
Door-to-door, door-to-fender, hood-to-fender. Uneven gaps are a common delivery issue.
Important
Paint — no chips, scratches, or swirl marks
Inspect in daylight or under bright service center lights. Check all four corners and hood leading edge.
Important
Exterior trim and badging — no peeling or misalignment
Run your finger along all trim pieces. Check Rivian lettering is straight and fully adhered.
Note
All doors open, close, and latch smoothly
Every door: frunk, tailgate (R1T), liftgate (R1S). Door alignment was an issue at my delivery.
Critical
Headlights and taillights — no condensation or damage
Check for moisture inside the housing. Test DRL, low beam, and high beam function.
Important
Lightbar illuminates evenly — front and rear
Gen 2 horizontal lightbar should light end to end with no dim zones or flickering.
Note
Horn works
Quick test in the lot. Also confirms steering wheel controls are functional.
Note
Wheels — no curb rash, correct spec
Verify wheel size and color match your order. Check all four for transport damage.
Important
Tires — correct spec, no sidewall damage, proper pressure
Check sidewalls for bulging or cuts. Verify PSI. Check for uneven wear.
Important
Undercarriage — no shipping straps or transport blocks left on
Crouch and look under all four corners. Transport hardware occasionally gets left on during delivery.
Critical
Frunk — opens, closes, latches, seal intact
Check the rubber seal around the perimeter. Water intrusion into the frunk has been reported.
Important
Charge port opens, closes, and accepts a cable
Test manually and via the Rivian app. Try plugging in a cable if one is available.
Critical
Cameras — clean lenses, no protective film left on
Check all Gear Guard cameras. Verify they show a clean image in the app.
Note
Air suspension — raises and lowers on all four corners
Cycle through heights in vehicle settings. Watch for any corner slow to respond.
Important
Glass & Wipers
Windshield, windows, wipers
0/4
Windshield — no chips, cracks, or delamination
Inspect at an angle to catch chips and star cracks. Check edges for delamination (white haze or bubbling).
Critical
Side and rear glass — no chips, cracks, or scratches
Run your eye along each pane. Check the liftgate glass on R1S carefully.
Important
Wiper blades — no streaking, correct fitment
Run wipers at all speeds with washer fluid spray. Check for streaking or skipping.
Note
Washer fluid reservoir has fluid
Spray front and rear washers if equipped. Low or empty fluid on a new vehicle is not uncommon.
Note
Interior
Trim, seats, materials, and storage
0/11
Wood / trim panels — no defects, gaps, or misalignment
A defect in my Dark Ash Wood trim was caught at delivery. Run your hand along all trim edges.
Important
Seats — no tears, stains, or stitching defects
Check all rows, especially seat bolsters — a common spot for cosmetic defects.
Important
Seat adjustments — all powered seats move in every direction
Test every direction on all adjustable seats. Check memory seat positions if equipped.
Important
Seat belts — retract and latch on all rows
Active recall on some 2022–2026 R1S/R1T for improperly tightened seat belt retractor bolts. Verify addressed before delivery.
Critical
Dynamic glass roof — tints, clears, and transitions evenly
Test full tint, partial tint, and clear mode. Verify the transition is even with no stuck zones.
Important
All windows — operate smoothly without grinding or rattling
Test every window. Check for slow operation or noise in the door channels.
Note
USB-C ports — test all with your device
Bring a cable. Verify front console, rear seat, and any other USB-C ports charge. Dead ports are easy to miss.
Important
Wireless charging pad working
Place your phone on the pad and confirm it charges. Check surface for scratches.
Note
Center console — no gaps, pass-through slot functional
Test the pass-through slot and all storage areas. Confirm the console lid doesn't bind.
Note
No unusual odors
Mold, chemical, or musty smells can indicate water intrusion during transport. Trust your nose.
Important
Spare tire kit present (if ordered)
Verify it's in place with all hardware included.
Note
Tech & Software
Screens, connectivity, keys, and app pairing
0/9
Main touchscreen — no dead zones, artifacts, or backlight bleed
Tap all corners and edges. Look for unresponsive areas or display issues.
Critical
Driver display — functioning and accurate
Verify speed, range, and navigation display correctly. Check for pixel issues or dead spots.
Important
Rivian app paired and connected
Verify the vehicle appears in your app. Test remote lock/unlock and horn honk.
Critical
Software on current version
Check Settings → About. If significantly behind, ask the service center to update before you leave.
Important
Bluetooth and phone key working
Test phone key unlock and start. Pair a second phone if you have a co-driver.
Important
Key fob / keycards included and working
Test fob lock, unlock, and programmable buttons. Verify keycards are in the delivery package.
Important
All speakers working — no distortion
Play music at moderate volume, check each zone. For Sound + Vision: test Dolby Atmos content.
Note
Driver assist / Rivian Autonomy Platform engages cleanly
On a short drive, verify the system activates and disengages without errors.
Note
Gear Guard enabled and cameras visible in live view
If you brought a USB-C SSD, set up Gear Guard storage during delivery.
Note
HVAC & Climate
Heating, cooling, and airflow
0/5
Heat produces warm air within a few minutes
An issue at my delivery — heater took 20+ minutes. Set to max heat and time it.
Critical
A/C cools properly from all vents
Run A/C on max for a few minutes. Verify cold air from front and rear vents.
Important
All vents direct airflow correctly
Test each zone including rear rows. Check middle-row vents respond to climate controls.
Note
Heated seats work on all rows
Test all heat levels on front seats. Check rear heated seats if equipped.
Note
No unusual HVAC noises at any fan speed
Run at various speeds and listen for rattling or grinding. Some owners report loud compressor sounds.
Note
Charging & Range
Battery, range, and charging setup
0/5
Battery state of charge matches expected delivery level
Most deliveries are at 50–80%. Verify displayed range is reasonable for your battery config.
Important
3 months free Adventure Network charging active in app
If you used a referral code, verify the free charging period shows under Benefits in your Rivian account.
Important
Mobile charging cable included and undamaged
Rivian includes a mobile charging cable. Verify it's present and undamaged.
Important
Referral code rewards points credited
Points may not appear immediately — check within 2 weeks. Contact Rivian support if missing after that.
Note
Charging schedule configured in app
Set up your home charging schedule before leaving. Configure off-peak hours if your utility offers TOU rates.
Note
Documentation
Paperwork and records
0/4
Window sticker / Monroney label saved
Photograph it or save via Rivian Roamer VIN lookup. Useful for warranty and insurance.
Note
Any pre-existing issues photographed and in app
Do this before leaving. Documentation at delivery carries far more weight than claims filed after the fact.
Critical
Service request filed for any noted issues
Open a service request before leaving. The delivery tech can often add notes on the spot.
Critical
VIN recorded
Note your VIN for insurance and recall lookups. Found on the driver door jamb and dashboard.

✓ Inspection Complete
All 52 items checked. File any open service requests before you drive away — and enjoy the truck.

From my delivery: Using a checklist caught three issues I would have missed — two misaligned doors, a defect in the Dark Ash Wood trim, and an HVAC problem where the heater took over 20 minutes to produce warm air. Rivian's app-based service request system made documenting everything fast and straightforward.

If you find issues, don't panic. Rivian expects some and the process for getting them fixed is clear. The key is documenting everything before you drive away.
