Charging tips from a real owner
Daily charge limit: 70–80%
Rivian recommends keeping daily charging below 80% for long-term battery health. Reserve 100% charges for road trips before a long drive.
Pre-condition before DC fast charging
Use the Rivian app to navigate to a fast charger — it automatically warms or cools the battery to optimal temperature, which can meaningfully improve charge speeds especially in cold weather.
Stop at 80% on road trips
Charging slows significantly above 80% due to battery protection tapering. On a long drive, stopping at 80% and moving to the next charger is almost always faster than waiting for 100%.
