How Rivian Referral Codes Work

Rivian owners receive a unique referral code that can be shared with new buyers through Rivian’s official Rivian Rewards program. When a qualifying vehicle order is placed using a referral code, both the buyer and the referring owner may receive Rivian Rewards points and complimentary Rivian Adventure Network charging.

Reward amounts vary based on vehicle configuration and battery selection. Current qualifying purchases can earn up to 500 Rivian Rewards points plus 3 months of Rivian Adventure Network charging.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I forgot to use a referral code?

If you forgot to apply a referral code during the ordering process, your Rivian sales guide can usually add it before vehicle delivery.

When are Rivian Rewards points added?

Rivian Rewards points are typically added to both the buyer and referring owner accounts within approximately two weeks after delivery.

How can Rivian Rewards points be used?

Rewards points may be redeemed toward products in the Rivian Gear Shop, R1 Shop, and select eligible purchases available through your Rivian account.

Do Rivian Rewards points expire?

Yes. Points currently expire two years after they are added to your Rivian account.

How does the Rivian Adventure Network charging reward work?

Complimentary Rivian Adventure Network charging is automatically tied to your Rivian account. Simply plug in and charge with no additional steps.

Eligible owners can accumulate up to 36 total months of Rivian Adventure Network charging rewards.

Can Rivian Rewards points be used toward service or accessories?

Rewards points may be applied toward select Rivian accessories and eligible installed products such as wheel and tire packages, wireless chargers, and powered tonneau covers.

Is this an official Rivian website?

No. Rivian Reward Hub is an independent owner-operated community resource and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Rivian Automotive, Inc.
