How it works

Referral rewards, explained

Rivian's official Rewards program lets owners share referral codes. When you place a qualifying order using a code, both you and the referring owner receive Rivian Rewards points and complimentary Adventure Network charging.

Points are added to your account within approximately two weeks of delivery and are valid for two years. Just enter the code during configuration — or ask your Rivian Guide to add it before delivery.
