How Rivian Referral Codes Work

Rivian owners receive a referral code that can be shared with new buyers through Rivian’s official Rivian Rewards program. When a qualifying Rivian order is placed using a referral code, both the buyer and referring owner may receive Rivian Rewards points and complimentary Rivian Adventure Network charging.

Current qualifying purchases can earn up to 500 Rivian Rewards points plus 3 months of Rivian Adventure Network charging depending on configuration and battery selection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I forgot to use a referral code?

Rivian sales guides can usually add a referral code before delivery if it was missed during ordering.

When are Rivian Rewards points added?

Points are generally added within approximately two weeks after delivery.

Do Rivian Rewards points expire?

Yes. Points currently expire two years after they are added to your Rivian account.

How does Rivian Adventure Network charging work?

Simply plug in and charge. Complimentary charging is automatically connected to your Rivian account.

Is this an official Rivian website?

No. Rivian Reward Hub is an independent owner-operated website and is not affiliated with Rivian Automotive, Inc.
