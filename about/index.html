About The Site

Built by a real Rivian owner.

Rivian Rewards Hub was created to share real-world ownership experiences, charging impressions, reviews, and useful resources for current and future Rivian owners.

Why I Created This Site

After ordering my Rivian R1S, I spent months researching ownership experiences, charging, accessories, software updates, and delivery expectations.

I found a lot of useful information scattered across YouTube videos, Reddit threads, forums, and Facebook groups, but there wasn’t one simple place that combined practical ownership information with a cleaner and more straightforward experience.

Rivian Rewards Hub started as a simple referral page, but quickly evolved into a broader ownership-focused site where I can document long-term experiences and share resources that I personally found helpful.

About Me

I’m based in Las Vegas and use the R1S as both a daily driver and a family vehicle. Most driving is pavement-focused, but the vehicle will also be used for road trips throughout the Southwest including Zion, Big Bear, and the Eastern Sierra.

Prior vehicles included a Jeep Grand Cherokee 4xe and a Land Rover Discovery, so I came into Rivian ownership with realistic expectations about modern vehicle reliability and technology.

Outside of Rivian ownership, I also have experience in photography and graphic design, which heavily influenced the visual direction and overall feel of the site.
